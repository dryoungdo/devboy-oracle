## Lessons Learned
1. **Explicit tool authorization flips the over-production rule.** "Don't spawn heavy work on vague directives" ≠ "never spawn." When a commander names `/workflows`, use it — sized to the task. (Counterpoint to yesterday's 11-agent pull.)
2. **Workflow `result` is nested under `result` in the output file** — parse `json.load(...)['result']` (may itself be a JSON string), not the top-level keys.
3. **desire(object) is polymorphic across business lenses, not just artist DNAs.** Gucci (heritage-maximalist) and Tesla×Porsche (engineered-minimalist) are opposite implementations of the same interface — the morph framework generalizes to brand genomes.
4. **For a tech object, luxury = subtraction.** Earned taper (form from physics), one alive accent at the periphery, machined hairline as the only ornament, monochrome, software-first permanence — NO gold/chrome.
5. **arra_learn: draft the pattern text first, then call** (gate against the recurring empty-call error).
