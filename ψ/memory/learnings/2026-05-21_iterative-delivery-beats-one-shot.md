# Iterative Delivery > One-Shot Perfection

Proven in session 5158a157 (article 034 Claude for Finance):
- Round 1 (134 lines): shallow surface coverage
- Round 2 (475 lines): expert depth but too dense, too many tables
- Round 3 (484 lines): restructured Part A practical + Part B deep dive, added Google Sheets, Thai+English Quick Wins

Captain's feedback drove each round. The final article was better than any single-shot attempt because each round addressed a different dimension: breadth → depth → usability.

**Structure that works:** Part A (practical, read-first) + Part B (deep dive, optional). Opening line: "ถ้ามีเวลาน้อย อ่าน Part A ก็พอ." This respects different reader needs.
