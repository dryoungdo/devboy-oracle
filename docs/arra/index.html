Manifesto
An intelligence you can hold. Carved from a single billet, silent until it speaks — the model upgrades, the object stays.

Most devices shout to feel premium. ARRA does the opposite. Everything you could admire from across the room has been stripped away, until what remains is worth picking up and examining. No gold. No spectacle. Just engineered restraint — and one alive display that holds the whole personality of the machine.

Design genome — Tesla × Porsche
Luxury, by subtraction.
01

Single-billet monolith

CNC-milled from one block of 6061-T6 aluminium. No visible screws, no panel gaps, no seams — fastening is internal, surfaces run unbroken.

02

Earned taper

Wide at the thermal base, narrowing to the display crown. The silhouette follows the heat stack inside — form from physics, not styling. The side profile is the hero.

03

One display, the soul

A single 4″ OLED set at a fixed read angle. No status arrays, no indicator lights. When it sleeps, the screen reads as matte aluminium continuation.

04

Calm accent glow

One cool-white light, in a machined undercut at the base — a soft halo on the desk, never glaring up at you. Three states. No RGB.

05

Machined precision

A single polished chamfer hairline on a pearl-blasted field — two textures, one material, one anodizing run. The reward for close inspection.

06

Software-first

The compute module is socketed, tool-free replaceable. It runs better intelligence in eighteen months without replacement. The object appreciates.
